The Technique

What is
Body Alignment?

Body Alignment is a South African-developed complementary health technique that promotes the body's natural capacity to heal and realign itself — without medication, surgery, or pain.

Stress, poor posture, and emotional or physical trauma cause muscle spasms that pull the skeleton out of alignment. This misalignment creates pressure on nerves and blood vessels, resulting in pain, discomfort, and reduced organ function.

Through gentle, targeted stimulation, muscles relax and release deep-held tension — allowing the skeletal system to naturally return to proper alignment and optimal function.

1
Assessment — Charné evaluates your posture, movement, and areas of tension to understand the root cause of your discomfort.
2
Targeted stimulation — Light, firm pressure is applied to specific muscle groups concentrating on the spinal column.
3
Natural realignment — As muscles relax, the skeleton realigns and nerve communication is restored throughout the body.

Results You Can Feel

Conditions that respond beautifully to treatment

Neck & Shoulders

Misalignment in the cervical spine may contribute to chronic headaches, dizziness, migraines, facial pain, and tension in the neck and shoulder area.

Lower Back & Hips

Misalignment in the lumbar spine may lead to sciatica, sacroiliac joint pain, hip and knee problems, and even digestive or bladder discomfort.

Elbow & Arms

Misalignment of the elbow joint may be the root cause of tennis elbow, carpal tunnel syndrome, and pins and needles or numbness in the fingers and hands.

Headaches & Migraines

Tension stored in the upper cervical muscles and the base of the skull is a common contributor to recurring headaches — often relieved through realignment.

Sciatica & Nerve Pain

Pressure on the sciatic nerve from pelvic or lumbar misalignment causes pain, burning, and numbness radiating down the leg — often dramatically relieved with Body Alignment.

Stress & Fatigue

Chronic stress stores itself physically in your muscles. Releasing this tension through Body Alignment restores nerve flow, energy levels, and overall sense of wellbeing.

Also effective for: postural problems · sports injuries · joint pain · digestive issues · ankle & knee pain · restless legs

Your Experience

What happens during a session?

Consultation & History

We begin with a thorough discussion of your health history, lifestyle, and specific areas of discomfort. This guides the entire session.

Postural Assessment

Charné evaluates your posture and movement patterns to identify areas where tension is pulling the spine and joints out of alignment.

Gentle Stimulation

You remain fully clothed while Charné applies light, precise pressure to key muscle groups — predominantly along the spinal column — encouraging deep muscle release.

Natural Realignment

As tension releases, the skeleton naturally returns to alignment. Many clients feel immediate relief and a profound sense of relaxation.

Aftercare Guidance

You receive personalised advice on posture, lifestyle, and any additional nutritional or herbal support to maintain your results between sessions.
