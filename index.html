THE TECHNIQUE

What is
Body Alignment?

Body Alignment is a complementary health technique that promotes the body’s capacity for self-healing.

Stress, poor posture and trauma cause muscle spasms that pull the skeleton out of alignment. This creates pressure on nerves and blood vessels, resulting in pain and discomfort.

Through gentle stimulation the muscles relax, heal and release deep tension, allowing the body to realign naturally.
