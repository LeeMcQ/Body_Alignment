The Technique

What is Body Alignment?

A South African-developed complementary health technique that promotes the body's natural capacity to heal and realign itself — without medication, surgery, or pain.

Stress, poor posture, and emotional or physical trauma cause muscle spasms that pull the skeleton out of alignment. This misalignment creates pressure on nerves and blood vessels, resulting in pain, discomfort, and reduced organ function.

Through gentle, targeted stimulation, muscles relax and release deep-held tension — allowing the skeletal system to naturally return to proper alignment and optimal function.

  1. 1

    Assessment

    Charné evaluates your posture, movement, and areas of tension to understand the root cause of your discomfort.

  2. 2

    Targeted Stimulation

    Light, firm pressure is applied to specific muscle groups concentrating on the spinal column.

  3. 3

    Natural Realignment

    As muscles relax, the skeleton realigns and nerve communication is restored throughout the body.

Body Alignment addresses the root cause of pain — spinal and skeletal misalignment — rather than just managing symptoms.

Neck & Shoulders

Misalignment in the cervical spine may contribute to chronic headaches, dizziness, migraines, facial pain, and tension in the neck and shoulder area.

Lower Back & Hips

Misalignment in the lumbar spine may lead to sciatica, sacroiliac joint pain, hip and knee problems, and even digestive or bladder discomfort.

Elbow & Arms

Misalignment of the elbow joint may be the root cause of tennis elbow, carpal tunnel syndrome, and pins and needles or numbness in the fingers and hands.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

Body Alignment is a gentle and accessible therapy for most people. Here are answers to the questions Charné is asked most often.

Is Body Alignment painful?

No — Body Alignment is intentionally gentle. The technique uses light but firm pressure on specific muscles. Most clients find it deeply relaxing, and many fall asleep on the table. There is no manipulation, cracking, or invasive contact.

How many sessions will I need?

Many clients feel significant improvement after 1–3 sessions. Chronic or long-standing conditions may benefit from a series of sessions. Charné will assess your individual needs and give you a clear recommendation during your first visit.

Do I need to undress?

No. You remain fully clothed throughout the entire session. Simply wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing for your appointment.

Is it safe for children and the elderly?

Yes — Body Alignment is extremely gentle and safe for all ages, from infants to the elderly. It is also safe during pregnancy. Charné adapts the pressure and approach for each individual client.

How do I book an appointment?

Simply WhatsApp or call Charné on 061 515 8052. Appointments are available Monday to Friday and are by arrangement only. She will confirm a time that suits you and send you the address details. You can also submit the enquiry form below.

Does it replace medical treatment?

Body Alignment is a complementary health technique and works alongside — not in place of — conventional medical care. Always consult your doctor for any medical conditions or concerns. Charné will refer you to appropriate medical professionals when needed.
