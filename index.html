The Technique

What is Body Alignment?

Body Alignment is a South African-developed complementary health technique that promotes the body's natural capacity to heal and realign itself — without medication, surgery, or pain.

Stress, poor posture, and emotional or physical trauma cause muscle spasms that pull the skeleton out of alignment. Through gentle, targeted stimulation, muscles relax and release deep-held tension — allowing the skeletal system to naturally return to proper alignment and optimal function.

1

Assessment

Charné evaluates your posture, movement, and areas of tension to understand the root cause of your discomfort.

2

Targeted Stimulation

Light, firm pressure is applied to specific muscle groups concentrating on the spinal column.

3

Natural Realignment

As muscles relax, the skeleton realigns and nerve communication is restored throughout the body.

Results You Can Feel

Conditions that respond beautifully to treatment

Neck & Shoulders

Misalignment in the cervical spine may contribute to chronic headaches, dizziness, migraines, facial pain, and tension.

Lower Back & Hips

Misalignment in the lumbar spine may lead to sciatica, sacroiliac joint pain, hip and knee problems.

Elbow & Arms

Misalignment of the elbow joint may cause tennis elbow, carpal tunnel syndrome, and pins and needles.

Headaches & Migraines

Tension stored in the upper cervical muscles is a common contributor to recurring headaches.

Sciatica & Nerve Pain

Pressure on the sciatic nerve from pelvic misalignment causes radiating pain — often dramatically relieved.

Stress & Fatigue

Chronic stress stores itself physically in your muscles. Releasing this tension restores nerve flow and energy.

Also effective for: postural problems · sports injuries · joint pain · digestive issues · ankle & knee pain · restless legs
